## Standard Data Room Structure

Every project follows the same 12-section architecture:

| # | Section | Purpose |
|---|---------|---------|
| 1 | Overview | TL;DR, key metrics, risk summary |
| 2 | Executive Memo | 1-page investment thesis |
| 3 | Product Vision | What it is, how it works, why it matters |
| 4 | Brand Strategy | Positioning, messaging, visual identity |
| 5 | Business Model | Revenue streams, pricing, unit economics |
| 6 | Market Analysis | TAM/SAM/SOM, trends, timing |
| 7 | Competitive Landscape | Competitors, positioning map, moats |
| 8 | Financial Model | Projections, P&L, cost structure |
| 9 | ROI Model & Scenarios | Conservative/Base/Aggressive, sensitivity |
| 10 | OKRs | 30-day + 90-day objectives |
| 11 | Pitch Deck | Slide outline or linked deck |
| 12 | Data Room Checklist | What's complete, partial, or missing |
