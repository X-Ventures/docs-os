# XLabs OKRs — 90 Days (Scaling and Repeatability)

## O1 — Factory Maturity

Prove the system works repeatably across 3+ simultaneous projects.

| Key Result | Target | Frequency |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| 100% projects follow standard pipeline | 100% | Weekly |
| Time idea-to-MVP < 5 weeks | < 5 weeks | Monthly |
| Pipeline completion rate > 80% | > 80% | Monthly |
| Playbook v2 published | v2 | Monthly |
| 3 reusable project templates extracted | 3 | Monthly |

## O2 — Intern Pipeline

Scale recruiting and maintain high success rate.

| Key Result | Target | Frequency |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| 10 interns onboarded over quarter | 10 | Monthly |
| Retention > 85% | > 85% | Monthly |
| Satisfaction > 8/10 | > 8/10 | Monthly |
| Velocity > 7 features/week (week 4+) | > 7/week | Weekly |
| Onboarding reduced to 5 days | 5 days | Monthly |

## O3 — Project Output (MVPs and Revenue)

Ship real products that generate traction.

| Key Result | Target | Frequency |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| 5+ MVPs deployed to production | 5 | Monthly |
| 2+ projects with > 50 MAU | 2 projects | Monthly |
| 1+ project generating revenue | 1 project | Monthly |
| 100% MVPs pass QA gate | 100% | Weekly |
| Every project has investor deck | 5 decks | Monthly |

Run a distribution machine for each project.

| Key Result | Target | Frequency |

| Landing conversion > 5% | > 5% | Weekly |
