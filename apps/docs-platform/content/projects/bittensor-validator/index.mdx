## TL;DR

Bittensor is a decentralized machine learning network where validators evaluate miners across specialized subnets and earn **41% of emissions**. The Executive Memo recommends a **90-day pilot** with a hybrid strategy — start with weight copying, then transition to real validation.

**Key insight:** ROI depends **70%+ on TAO price appreciation**, not yield (~5% APY). Staking yield alone barely covers infrastructure costs at small scale. With 5,000 TAO in a Bull scenario, total ROI is **+69%**.

The recommended approach (Strategy C — Hybrid) starts at €3,200/mo OPEX and breaks even operationally at ~7,100 TAO. Below 3,500 TAO, no strategy is profitable on yield alone.

## Key Risk Summary

| Risk | Severity | Impact |

## Decision Framework

The board needs to answer three questions:

1. **Do we believe TAO appreciates 20%+ over 12 months?** If yes, the validator is a leveraged bet on TAO with infrastructure optionality. If no, yield alone does not justify the OPEX.
2. **Can we commit 5,000+ TAO?** Below this threshold, operational losses are near-certain. At 1,500 TAO, every strategy loses money.
3. **Do we have (or can we hire) ML/infra expertise?** The hybrid strategy buys time, but real validation requires a dedicated operator within 3-6 months.
