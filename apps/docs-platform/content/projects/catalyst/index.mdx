## TL;DR (2-Minute Overview)

Catalyst is a **deal flow operating system for Web3 investors and founders**. Think Bloomberg Terminal meets AngelList, purpose-built for crypto venture. Founders upload pitch decks and get AI-powered analysis from 10 simulated VC personas (PitchScan). Investors access curated, AI-scored deal flow filtered by vertical, stage, and quality. The platform includes competitive intelligence (Surf AI), a CRM for fundraising, tokenomics tools, and 6 automated agents that scan the market 24/7.

**Business model**: Freemium SaaS (Founder Free/Pro $29/Enterprise $99 + Investor Free/Pro $300/Institutional $1,000) plus transaction fees, promoted placements, data products, and partner revenue share.

**Competitive edge**: Only platform combining AI pitch analysis + curated Web3 deal flow + competitive intelligence + CRM in one product. 6,500+ investor database gives immediate distribution advantage.
