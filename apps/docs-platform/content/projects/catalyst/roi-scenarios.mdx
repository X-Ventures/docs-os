# Catalyst — ROI Model & Scenarios

---

## Three Scenarios (Year 3)

| Metric | Conservative | Base | Aggressive |
|--------|-------------|------|-----------|
| **Paid Founders** | 500 | 2,000 | 5,000 |
| **Paid Investors** | 20 | 100 | 300 |
| **MRR** | $40K | $180K | $500K+ |
| **ARR** | $500K | $2.2M | $6M+ |
| **Transaction Revenue** | $100K | $1M | $5M+ |
| **Total Revenue** | $1M | $5M | $15M+ |
| **EBITDA Margin** | -10% | 15% | 30%+ |
| **Team Size** | 5 | 15 | 30 |

---

## Assumptions

| Assumption | Conservative | Base | Aggressive |
|-----------|-------------|------|-----------|
| Free-to-Paid Conversion | 3% | 6% | 10% |
| Investor ARPU | $300/mo | $500/mo | $700/mo |
| Founder ARPU | $29/mo | $50/mo | $75/mo |
| Monthly Churn | 5% | 3% | 2% |
| Deals Closed via Platform | 10 | 50 | 150 |
| Avg Deal Size | $1M | $2M | $3M |
| Success Fee Rate | 1% | 1.5% | 2% |

## Sensitivity Analysis

### Impact of Conversion Rate on ARR (Year 2)

| Conversion Rate | Paid Users | MRR | ARR |
|----------------|-----------|-----|-----|
| 2% | 200 | $15K | $180K |
| 5% | 500 | $38K | $456K |
| 8% | 800 | $60K | $720K |
| 12% | 1,200 | $90K | $1.08M |

### Impact of Investor ARPU on Revenue

| ARPU | 50 Investors | 100 Investors | 200 Investors |
|------|-------------|--------------|--------------|
| $300/mo | $180K/yr | $360K/yr | $720K/yr |
| $500/mo | $300K/yr | $600K/yr | $1.2M/yr |
| $1,000/mo | $600K/yr | $1.2M/yr | $2.4M/yr |

## Break-Even Analysis

| Scenario | Monthly OPEX | Required MRR | Required Paid Users |
|----------|-------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Lean (3 people) | $20K | $20K | 70 founders + 5 investors |
| Growth (8 people) | $60K | $60K | 150 founders + 15 investors |
| Scale (15 people) | $120K | $120K | 300 founders + 30 investors |
