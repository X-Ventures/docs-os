## Competitor Analysis

### Deal Flow Platforms (AngelList, Republic)

- **Strong**: Large networks, established brand, SPV infrastructure
- **Weak**: Not Web3-focused, no AI analysis, no competitive intelligence
- **Catalyst edge**: Web3 specialization + PitchScan AI + automated agents

### Data Providers (PitchBook, Crunchbase)

- **Strong**: Comprehensive databases, institutional trust
- **Weak**: Expensive ($30K+/yr for PitchBook), no AI analysis, passive data
- **Catalyst edge**: 100x cheaper, AI-native, active deal flow not just data

### AI Pitch Analysis (Deckmatch, emerging tools)

- **Strong**: Novel AI approaches to deck scoring
- **Weak**: Scoring only, no ecosystem (no deal flow, no CRM, no agents)
- **Catalyst edge**: Full platform (analysis + deal flow + CRM + intelligence)

### Web3 Data (Messari, Nansen, Dune)

- **Strong**: Deep on-chain analytics, crypto-native
- **Weak**: Data only, no deal flow, no founder tools, no pitch analysis
- **Catalyst edge**: Connects data to deal flow and decision-making

### VC CRM (Affinity, 4Degrees)

- **Strong**: Relationship intelligence, workflow automation
- **Weak**: No AI analysis, no deal sourcing, expensive, not Web3
- **Catalyst edge**: CRM + AI analysis + deal sourcing in one product

### DocSend

- **Strong**: Industry standard for deck sharing, view tracking
- **Weak**: No AI analysis, no deal flow, acquired by Dropbox
- **Catalyst edge**: PitchScan replaces the need for basic view tracking with deep analysis

## Unique Position

Catalyst is the **only platform combining all four pillars**:

| Pillar | What It Means |
|--------|--------------|
| AI Analysis | 10-persona pitch analysis, not just scoring |
| Deal Flow | Two-sided marketplace (founders + investors) |
| Intelligence | Competitive intel, market scanning, automated agents |
| Web3 Native | Tokenomics, on-chain data, crypto-specific workflows |

No competitor has more than 2 of these 4 pillars.
