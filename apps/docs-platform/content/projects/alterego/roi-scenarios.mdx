## Key Assumptions

- Hardware COGS declines 8% annually with scale
- Subscription churn stabilizes at 3% monthly by month 6
- Marketplace take rate: 30% on all skill transactions
- No additional fundraising modeled (seed only)
- Enterprise revenue begins Q3 of Year 1
