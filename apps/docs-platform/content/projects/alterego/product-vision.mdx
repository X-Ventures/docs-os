# AlterEgo — Product Vision

> Confidential | February 2026 | Auto-synced from Google Drive

## One Device. One Mind. Yours.

AlterEgo exists because AI deserves better than a chat window. Today, the most powerful technology ever created is trapped inside the same device that sends you push notifications, tracks your location, and sells your attention. AlterEgo changes that.

AlterEgo is a **dedicated physical AI agent** — a small, beautifully crafted orb that sits in your home, learns who you are, and works for you. It doesn't have social media, ads, or an app store designed to hijack your dopamine. It has one purpose: **to know you, and to help you.**

### Three Founding Principles

| Principle | What It Means |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Dedicated, Not Distracted** | No competing apps, no notifications, no feeds. When you speak to AlterEgo, it is fully present. |
| **Private by Architecture** | Your data never leaves your control. No training, no ads, no profiles. Privacy is the foundation, not a setting. |
| **Emotional, Not Transactional** | The avatar gives it a face. Physical presence gives it a place. Persistent memory gives it continuity. It's a relationship, not a tool. |

## The Device

**Form**: Premium spherical orb, ~4 inches diameter. Machined aluminum with recessed circular OLED touchscreen (2.5-inch). The screen sits within a concave face, creating depth — as if the AI lives inside.

**Finishes** (5 options):

| Finish | Material | Target Aesthetic |
|--------|----------|-----------------|
| Silver | Brushed aluminum | Industrial meets warmth (flagship) |
| White | Ceramic-feel matte | Minimalist, Scandinavian |
| Black | Anodized stealth | Bold, dramatic, design-forward |
| Rose Gold | Warm metallic | Soft, approachable, premium |
| Forest Green | Dark rich tone | Collector's statement piece |

**Audio**: High-fidelity speaker array, far-field microphone array with on-device voice detection, **physical mute switch** for absolute control.

**Processing**: Custom edge AI chipset for local inference. Wi-Fi for cloud access via encrypted, private API endpoints. No data stored on third-party servers.

**Screen Modes**:

| Mode | Description |
|------|------------|
| Avatar | Personalized AI face (default resting state) |
| App Grid | Apple Watch-style skill icons for quick navigation |
| Glance | Weather, calendar, notifications at a glance |
| Listening | Animated audio waveform during voice processing |
| Ambient | Subtle clock or visualization when idle |
| Privacy | Screen off, microphone physically muted, full blackout |

## OpenClaw OS

Purpose-built operating system. Skills are **whitelisted** — no open app store, no unreviewed code. Clean, minimal interface. Automatic silent updates. Multi-user support (up to 5 profiles via voice recognition).

**Private API Architecture**: All AI processing uses dedicated, encrypted endpoints. User data is never used for model training or shared with third parties.
